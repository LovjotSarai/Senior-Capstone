About Us

Canary Consultants is an accounting firm serving the metropolitan Washington DC, Greater Northern Virginia and Maryland areas. Our firm consists of board certified CPAs, Project Managers, IT Consultants and Cyber Security experts with extensive experience in the fields of accounting, financial management, and Cybersecurity. We provide value added services with a strong emphasis on professionalism and quality in an environment of integrity, maturity, and respect.

Our Mission is to achieve total client satisfaction by helping them make informed and timely business decisions. Canary Consultants are your trustworthy business and cyber security advisors. We strive towards progress as your business succeeds. We approach every situation with purpose and pragmatism, taking on your challenges as our own. We partner with our clients to prepare for change and deliver exceptional results.
